saturation_water_pressure¶

pycraf.atm.saturation_water_pressure(temp, press, wet_type='water')[source] [edit on github]¶

Saturation water pressure according to ITU-R P.453-12.

Parameters:

temp : Quantity

Air temperature [K]

press : Quantity

Total air pressure [hPa]

wet_type : str, optional

Type of wet material: ‘water’, ‘ice’
Returns:

press_sat : Quantity

Saturation water vapor pressure, e_s [hPa]
