eff_earth_radius_factor_beta¶

pycraf.pathprof.eff_earth_radius_factor_beta()[source] [edit on github]¶

Calculate effective Earth radius factor exceeded for beta_0 percent of time, k_beta, according to ITU-R P.452-16.

Returns:
k_beta : Quantity

Effective Earth radius factor exceeded for beta_0 percent of time [dimless]

Notes

  • This is just a constant. You could also use K_BETA to avoid overhead.
