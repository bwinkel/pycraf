powerflux_nu_from_t_a¶

pycraf.conversions.powerflux_nu_from_t_a(T_A, eff_area)[source] [edit on github]¶

Spectral power flux density from antenna temperature, given effective antenna area.

The spectral power flux density is the power flux density per unit frequency, i.e., powerflux_nu = powerflux / bandwidth.

Parameters:

T_A : Quantity

Antenna temperature [K]

eff_area : Quantity

Effective antenna area, A_eff [m**2]
Returns:

powerflux_nu : Quantity

Spectral power flux density [W/m**2/Hz, dB_W_m2_Hz, Jy]

Notes

Because all parameters/returned values are Astropy Quantities (see Quantity), unit conversion is automatically performed.
