antfactor_from_gain¶

pycraf.conversions.antfactor_from_gain(gain, freq, zi)[source] [edit on github]¶

Antenna factor from antenna gain, given frequency and impedance.

Parameters:

gain : Quantity

Antenna gain [dBi, or dimless]

freq : Quantity

Frequency [Hz]

zi : Quantity

Receiver impedance, Zi [Ohm]
Returns:

antfactor : Quantity

Antenna factor, Ka [dB(1/m)]

Notes

Because all parameters/returned values are Astropy Quantities (see Quantity), unit conversion is automatically performed.
