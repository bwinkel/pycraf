ptx_from_prx¶

pycraf.conversions.ptx_from_prx(prx, gtx, grx, dist, freq)[source] [edit on github]¶

Transmitted power from received power.

Parameters:

prx : Quantity

Received power, Prx [dB_W, W]

gtx : Quantity

Gain of transmitter, Gtx [dBi, dimless]

grx : Quantity

Gain of receiver, Grx [dBi, dimless]

dist : Quantity

Distance between transmitter and receiver [m]

freq : Quantity

Frequency of radiation [Hz]
Returns:

ptx : Quantity

Transmitter power, Ptx [W]

Notes

Because all parameters/returned values are Astropy Quantities (see Quantity), unit conversion is automatically performed.
